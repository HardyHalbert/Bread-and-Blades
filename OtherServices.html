Services

Everything we do here in Bread and Blades!

Weapon Forging

At Bread and Blades, we offer the unique opportunity to create your own weapon through our Weapon Forging service. Our experienced blacksmiths will guide you through the process, from selecting the right materials to hammering out the perfect shape. Whether you want to create a one-of-a-kind sword, axe, or mace, our Weapon Forging service is perfect for any aspiring warrior or collector.

Baking Goods

Our Baking Goods service at Bread and Blades offers a wide range of delicious and freshly baked treats. From artisan bread to pastries and cakes, we use only the finest ingredients to create baked goods that are sure to delight your taste buds. Our selection is constantly changing, so be sure to stop by and see what we have in store.

Baking Classes

If you want to learn the art of baking, our Baking Classes are the perfect opportunity. Our experienced bakers will teach you everything you need to know, from mastering basic techniques to creating complex desserts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced baker, our classes are tailored to your skill level, so you can develop your skills and learn something new.

Blacksmithing Classes

Our Blacksmithing Classes are the perfect opportunity to learn the art of metalworking. From creating tools to forging weapons, our experienced blacksmiths will guide you through the process, teaching you the necessary techniques and safety precautions. Whether you're interested in making a career out of blacksmithing or just want to try something new, our classes are a great way to get started.

Catering

Our Catering service at Bread and Blades offers delicious food and professional service for any occasion. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or private party, we will work with you to create a menu that perfectly suits your needs and tastes. With our attention to detail and commitment to quality, you can be sure that your event will be a success.

Gamenight

Looking for a fun and social activity? Join us for Gamenight at Bread and Blades! We offer a wide selection of board games, card games, and role-playing games, along with snacks and drinks to keep you fueled throughout the night. Our friendly staff will help you choose a game and get started, so all you have to do is relax and have fun.
