Privacy Policy for Bread and Blades Website

At Bread and Blades, we take the privacy of our website visitors seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we may collect from our visitors, how we use that information, and the steps we take to safeguard it.

Information We Collect When you visit our website, we may collect certain information about you. This may include your IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages you visit on our website. If you choose to contact us through our website, we may collect your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide to us.

Use of Information We use the information we collect from our visitors to improve the content and functionality of our website, respond to inquiries and requests, and provide better customer service. We may also use this information to analyze how visitors use our website and to inform our marketing efforts.

Information Sharing We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, but only to the extent necessary for them to perform their services.

Security We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect from our visitors. We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Cookies We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. A cookie is a small file that is placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. It enables the website to remember your actions and preferences over time. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

Links to Other Websites Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any website you visit.

Changes to this Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the date at the top of the policy.

Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@breadandblades.com.
